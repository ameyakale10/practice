Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions

What is roadmap.sh? ▼

roadmap.sh is a site that provides learning paths and resources for developers.

How do I use the roadmaps? ▼

You follow the roadmap steps in order, learning each topic and building projects as you go.

Is roadmap.sh free? ▼

Yes, the roadmaps and most of the resources are free to use.
